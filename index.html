Property Overview

Located at 35 Longwestgate, Scarborough, North Yorkshire, YO11 1QB, The Old Town Cottage is a historic mid-terrace property offering a unique blend of coastal charm and modern amenities. Enjoy the beauty of Scarborough with its rich maritime heritage and vibrant local culture.

  • Layout: Two main bedrooms (a first-floor double and a second-floor twin) and a fully equipped bathroom.
  • Living & Kitchen: A tidy kitchen/diner with modern appliances, paired with a comfortable sitting room featuring an electric fire, Smart TV, WiFi, and a collection of books and games.
  • Outdoor Space: A spacious decking area and garden perfect for alfresco dining and relaxation.

Additional features include a highchair and travel cot available on request, along with a pet-friendly policy (one well-behaved dog allowed). Check-in is from 4:00 pm and check-out by 10:00 am.

Charming Accommodation

Our cottage comfortably accommodates up to four guests in thoughtfully designed rooms:

  • Double Bedroom: Spacious and elegantly furnished on the first floor.
  • Twin Bedroom: Offering lovely views through charming roof windows.

The well-appointed bathroom features both a bath and a separate shower with contemporary fixtures.

Refined Living Spaces & Amenities

Our kitchen/diner is equipped with modern appliances that perfectly balance functionality and charm:

  • Electric oven and induction hob
  • Microwave and premium coffee machine
  • Spacious fridge/freezer combo
  • Washing machine and dishwasher

The cozy sitting room, complete with an electric fireplace, Smart TV, and high-speed WiFi, creates a relaxed, home-like ambiance.

Outdoor Elegance & Additional Features

Step outside to enjoy our serene decking area and garden—ideal for alfresco dining or simply unwinding by the sea.

  • Pet-friendly environment (one well-behaved dog allowed)
  • Family amenities including a highchair and travel cot on request
  • Complimentary roadside parking permit
