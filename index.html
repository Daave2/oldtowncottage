Property Overview

Located at 35 Longwestgate, Scarborough, United Kingdom, The Old Town Cottage is a charming historic mid-terrace property spanning three floors. Ideally situated near local restaurants, shops, and the beachfront—and just on the edge of the North York Moors National Park—it offers an exceptional base for exploring the area.

  • Layout: Two main bedrooms (a first-floor double and a second-floor twin arrangement) and a fully equipped bathroom (bath, shower, WC, and basin).
  • Living & Kitchen: A tidy kitchen/diner with modern appliances alongside a comfortable sitting room featuring an electric fire, Smart TV with Freeview, WiFi, and a selection of books and games.
  • Outdoor Space: Enjoy a spacious decking area and garden ideal for outdoor meals and relaxation.

Additional amenities include family-friendly extras (highchair and travel cot on request), a pet-friendly policy (one well-behaved dog allowed), and a convenient roadside parking permit. Check-in is from 4:00 pm with check-out by 10:00 am.

Exquisite Accommodation

Our cottage comfortably accommodates up to four guests in thoughtfully designed rooms:
